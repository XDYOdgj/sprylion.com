Enhance Feeding Experiences with Textured Magenta Spoons

These 12 bumpy Magenta spoons are specially designed to support children with challenges in lip closure or oral hypersensitivity. The shallow, tilted bowls help encourage an overbite, making it easier for kids to handle difficult textures while eating. Each spoon has an easy-to-grip handle, promoting comfort and oral awareness during feeding times.

Perfect for Every Feeding Need

Ideal for both preparing and serving solid foods, these spoons are versatile and user-friendly. They come with an included instructions booklet to guide caregivers through proper use, and a compact storage bag for easy portability. Whether at home, school, or during therapy sessions, these spoons make feeding more manageable and efficient.

Key Features

  • Promotes oral awareness and comfort
  • Dishwasher safe and BPA-free
  • Easy to grip for adults and children
  • Includes instructions and storage bag
